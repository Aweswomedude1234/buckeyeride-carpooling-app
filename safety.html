Your Safety is Our Priority

We're committed to creating a safe and trusted community for all users.

Community Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat all users with respect and courtesy. Discrimination, harassment, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.

Verify Your Identity

Complete your profile verification to build trust within the community. Verified users get better matches.

Communicate Clearly

Be honest about your plans, preferences, and any changes to your ride arrangements.

Respect Privacy

Don't share other users' personal information without their consent.

Safety Features

Profile Verification

All users can verify their identity with government-issued ID and driver's license (for drivers).

Ratings & Reviews

Rate your ride experience and read reviews from other users before accepting rides.

In-App Messaging

Communicate with other users without sharing personal contact information.

Emergency Contacts

Add emergency contacts who can be notified if you need help during a ride.

24/7 Support

Access our support team anytime through the app or website.

Incident Reporting

Report safety concerns or inappropriate behavior directly through the platform.

Tips for Passengers

Before the Ride

  • Check the driver's profile and ratings
  • Verify the vehicle matches the profile
  • Share ride details with a friend or family member
  • Meet in a public, well-lit location

During the Ride

  • Wear your seatbelt
  • Follow your route on your phone's map
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, speak up
  • Keep emergency contacts easily accessible

Tips for Drivers

Before the Ride

  • Verify your passenger's identity
  • Ensure your vehicle is clean and well-maintained
  • Check that your insurance is current
  • Plan your route in advance

During the Ride

  • Follow all traffic laws
  • Keep your phone mounted and use hands-free navigation
  • Be respectful and professional
  • Don't share personal contact information

Emergency Procedures

If You Feel Unsafe

  1. Contact local authorities immediately (911 in the US)
  2. Use the in-app emergency button if available
  3. Contact our 24/7 support team
  4. Report the incident through the app
