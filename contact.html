Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a safety concern?

If you experience or witness any safety issues, please contact us immediately at safety@rideconnect.com or use the in-app safety features.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from your profile settings, or contact support@rideconnect.com for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Payment is handled directly between users.

How do I become a verified driver?

Complete your profile verification in the app by providing your driver's license and vehicle information.
