Connecting Buckeyes, One Ride at a Time

BuckeyeRide is more than just a carpooling app – we're building a community of Ohio State students, faculty, and staff who believe in sustainable, affordable, and social transportation.

Our Mission

We believe that getting around campus and Columbus should be easy, affordable, and environmentally friendly. BuckeyeRide connects members of the Ohio State community through smart carpooling technology that prioritizes safety, compatibility, and convenience.

By matching riders based on detailed preferences and building lasting carpooling relationships, we're reducing traffic, lowering transportation costs, and creating meaningful connections within the Buckeye community.

How BuckeyeRide Works

1

Create Your Profile

Tell us about your preferences – music taste, smoking policy, pet-friendliness, and how chatty you like your rides. This helps us find your perfect carpool match.

2

Set Your Schedule

Add your regular routes and times. Whether it's daily commutes to campus, weekend trips home, or one-time events, we'll help you find compatible riders.

3

Find Your Match

Our smart matching system considers location, timing, and personal preferences to connect you with compatible carpoolers in your area.

4

Create Groups

Form carpooling groups with friends, classmates, or coworkers. Share schedules within your group and coordinate regular rides easily.

5

Build Buddy Networks

After great rides, send buddy requests to your favorite carpoolers. Buddies get priority matching and can easily coordinate future rides together.

Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. All BuckeyeRide users must verify their Ohio State affiliation, and we provide multiple safety features to ensure secure carpooling experiences.

  • OSU email verification required
  • Profile verification system
  • Ride tracking and check-in/check-out
  • Rating and review system
  • 24/7 support for safety concerns
  • Emergency contact integration
